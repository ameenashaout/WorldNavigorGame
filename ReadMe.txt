Welcome user to the navigator game

If you want to start just press your mouse on the start button.

If you want to move :
Press W from the keyboard to move up.
Press S from the keyboard to move down.
Press A from the keyboard to move left.
Press D from the keyboard to move right.

Make sure to take the key from the mirror.

Be careful about the time, you have two minutes.
You can stop playing any time you want by press your mouse on the stop game button,
you can complete play by press your mouse on the start game button.

Finally, open the treasure with your key in the final room to win.

Enjoy it.
